How to Play Level 1:
Your mission is to cleanse our city's air by skillfully avoiding harmful carbon emissions. To navigate, use the Spacebar to jump over incoming pollutants. Your score increases as you successfully dodge these environmental threats, and your goal is to reach a score of 300 to win the level. Be careful, though, as any collision with pollution will result in a game over!
