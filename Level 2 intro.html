How to Play Level 2:
Your mission is to protect the ground from falling pollution. Use the Left Arrow and Right Arrow keys to move your defender horizontally, and press the Spacebar to fire projectiles upwards. Earn points by successfully destroying the falling plastic garbage and smoke clouds. Be vigilant, as the game ends if any pollution reaches the ground, but if you manage to reach a score of 100, you win and secure the skies!
